Why Stuber?

It's quite simple really... we know how finacially tough student life can be. Cutting costs where you can is vital in order to scoff down your next pie at the cafe. So how do we make life easier for you? Student ride sharing. It's a fun and affordable way to commute.
While ride sharing isn't new, Stuber is a fresh way to share the road and the costs. Choose to drive or ride, and match with students based on route and time of day. Son't be stupid and Stuber today!
